About me

Welcome! I'm Siril Olsen Vaular, and I'm happy you've dropped by.

After working as a teacher for seven years, I've dived headfirst into the world of coding, and I love it! I'm 33-years of age, and currently immersed in frontend studies at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media. Besides being a true crime junkie, I have a keen interest in people, delectable food and wine, the great outdoors, and of course, coding and design ...

From crafting handbags out of old cloth scraps to developing engaging teaching plans, I've always been driven by the joy of creation and witnessing tangible results. Working with frontend, I get to combine my love for creativity, visual communication, and design with creating web solutions with user experience in mind.

My journey started from scratch as I dived into HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript with no prior coding experience. The projects you see on this site represent my first year of work. I'm eager to continue this journey, developing and soaking in every bit of experience along the way.

If you have questions, want to chat, or explore potential collaborations, feel free to reach out.
